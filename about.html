What is AYUSH?

AYUSH stands for Ayurveda, Yoga & Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha, and Homeopathy — traditional systems of medicine practiced in India for centuries. Each of these systems offers unique perspectives on health, wellness, and natural healing, complementing modern medicine.

Ayurveda: The Science of Life

Ayurveda is a holistic system of medicine that focuses on balance in the body, mind, and spirit, using natural remedies, diet, and lifestyle changes.

Yoga: Union of Body and Mind

Yoga is a spiritual and ascetic discipline that includes breath control, meditation, and specific bodily postures for health and relaxation.

Unani: The Art of Healing

Unani medicine is based on the principles of four humors and provides natural treatments with herbs, minerals, and animal products.

Siddha: The Ancient Medical System

Siddha medicine is a traditional medicine originating from Tamil Nadu in India that emphasizes natural healing methods and herbal medicine.

Homeopathy: The Gentle Healing

Homeopathy is a system of alternative medicine based on the principle of 'like cures like,' using highly diluted substances to trigger the body’s natural healing.
